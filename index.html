The Priming Effect — farming's hidden cost

Excess synthetic nitrogen causes soil microbes to consume stored soil carbon to balance their C:N diet. Every extra bag of urea is destroying the very soil structure that makes farms productive long-term.

−0.3% SOC/yr
Average loss rate under high-urea systems

Why the OBI matters: the Rabobank precedent

The Open Bodemindex (OBI) is already used by Rabobank Netherlands to assess farm loan risk. Developed by WUR (Wageningen University), a.s.r. insurance, and Vitens water company. NAITRO adapts this validated framework for NZ and SA conditions — making your soil health score bankable.
